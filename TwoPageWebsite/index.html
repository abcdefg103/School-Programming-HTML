My Favorite Tableware is a Plate
     Plates are an essential part of any major family food times, and a standard for restaurants. Plates are regarded as a sophisticated method of consuming food. It keeps the germs from the cold ground. Many people in the world put their food on a plate before consumption. It also helped many dishwasher companies grow. As the image shows, it is a flat disk-like object. It is very delicate, and can easily crack at a blink of an eye if not cared for properly. Although not a proper way of use, It has also been used often for decorative purposes, where people will put drawings on it and sell for high prices. I personally favor this dinner essential because I could eat it with many other foods, and I find myself using it over and over again.
