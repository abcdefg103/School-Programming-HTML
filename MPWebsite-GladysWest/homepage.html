Gladys West
The Hero Behind The GPS - Home
There is one thing that most people have used many times in their daily life. And that is the GPS(Global Positioning System). But have you ever thought about the creator of the GPS? Who allowed us all to use Google Maps to get to Los Angeles by car? That's Gladys West. A underappreciated colored woman who had contributed to the modern world crucially.


This website will show what she exactly did, how her life had led her there, and more.
